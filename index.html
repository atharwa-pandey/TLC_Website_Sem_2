Teaching Learning Centre
BITS Pilani - Pilani Campus

We develop pedagogies that promote
ACTIVE LEARNING CRITICAL THINKING TECH INNOVATIONS IN EDUCATION
“Tell me and I forget. Teach me and I remember. Involve me and I learn.”
— Benjamin Franklin
The Teaching-Learning Centre at Pilani Campus is envisioned
  • as an entity that fosters the teaching-learning experience
  • as an entity that enables the agency of both the learner and the teacher
  • as being grounded on the learner-centered paradigm of education
  • as a forum for discussing teaching pedagogy and evaluation techniques
  • as an entity that encourages innovation in teaching-learning methodologies
  • as an entity that promotes the use of the latest technologies in the teaching-learning process
  • as a forum for resolving challenges in the teaching-learning process through a collegial rather than a prescriptive approach
